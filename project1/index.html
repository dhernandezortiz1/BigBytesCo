Breakfast: Bagel Sandwich
recipe link

    Ingredients

  • 1 Bagel
  • 1 Egg
  • Cheese Slice(American or peronal choice)
  • 2 pieces of breafast meat (deli Ham, Bacon, or substitute)
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

    • Tools

  • Toaster

Steps:

  1. Place bagel in toaster and toast as desired.
  2. Over medium heat, fry egg in frying pan.
  3. Turn off heat but, leave pan on burner.
  4. Place meat on top of egg and then the cheese on top of the meat.
  5. Cover with a lid and let the heat melt the cheese.
  6. Butter toasted bagel.
  7. Place egg, meat, and cheese pile on one half of bagel and top with the other half.
  8. Slice in half and enjoy!
Lunch: Black bean quesadillas
recipe link

    Ingredients

  • 15oz black beans
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • 1/2 cup red onion
  • Fresh cilantro(optional)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • Taco seasoning
  • Flour tortillas

Steps:

  1. Drain the black beans and add them to a bowl along with the frozen corn (no need to thaw)
  2. Finely dice the onion, mince the garlic, and roughly chop the cilantro.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, cilantro, shredded cheddar, and taco seasoning to the bowl with the beans and corn. Stir until everything is evenly combined and coated in seasoning.
  4. Place a half cup of the filling on one side of each tortilla and fold over.
  5. Cook the quesadillas in a skillet over medium heat on each side until brown and crispy and the cheesy filling has melted.
  6. Slice into triangles then serve.
Upgraded Instant Ramen

    Ingredients

  • Instant Ramen pack(chicken flavor)
  • Rotisserie chicken
  • 1 Egg
  • Minced Garlic
  • Chopped scallions
  • Other vegetabkes of your choice
  • Seasame seeds and/or Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Cook instant noodles as per package directions (cook with boiling water)
  2. For soft boiled egg, cook in boiling water for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Chop rotisserie chicken into slices
  4. Pour cooked ramen into bowl
  5. Place chicken and sliced egg into bowl and add vegetables and scallions
  6. Top off with sesame seeds and/or cilantro
  7. Stir and enjoy!
Dessert: Brownie in a mug

    Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 2 tablesoons melted butter
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips [or bluberries]
  • [optional] icecream

    • Tools/Equipment

  • 12 oz mug

Steps:

  1. Into mug, add flour, sugar, and pinch of salt
  2. Mix until fully combined
  3. Add melted butter and milk into mug
  4. Mix until thick brownie batter forms
  5. Add blue berries/chocolate chips into batter
  6. Put in microwave on high for 1 minute and 20 seconds. If not fully cooked, microwave foranother 20 seconds.
  7. Use spoon to enjoy
